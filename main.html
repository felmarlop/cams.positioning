 Â¿How to build your building?

Save your polygon coordinates in a file. The order of which can be right or left but it is very important that they are ordered because each coordinate is related to the next. Every coordinate would be the corners of the building.

 Coordinate format

The x and y values in brackets and separated by commas. With decimal numbers you must split them by dots. Example: (5.4, 7.1)

 Supported extensions

TXT (Coming soon: CSV DXF ZIP RAR)

The purpose of the software is show graphically the optimal areas about any building where the user can have visibility of every wall and corner. Thereafter the user can export the data in PNG, PDF or SVG.
